The Story

Made by hand,
worn with meaning

"Every bead is chosen.
Every eye is placed where it can see."

n studio grew from a lifelong love of the nazar — the evil eye — and a refusal to let jewellery be dull. Each bracelet is made one at a time, with a glass evil eye bead at its centre and a carefully chosen mix of ceramic, stone, and glass beads around it.

No two are the same. When you wear one, you're wearing something that exists only once in the world — and something made to keep watch for you.

Design by Latife Nesrin · Based in London
