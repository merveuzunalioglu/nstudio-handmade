The Story

Made by hand,
worn with meaning

"Every bead is chosen.
Every eye is placed where it can see."

n studio grew from a lifelong love of the nazar — the evil eye — and a refusal to let jewellery be dull. Each bracelet is made one at a time in Istanbul, with a glass evil eye bead at its centre and a carefully chosen mix of ceramic, stone, and glass beads around it.

No two are the same. When you wear one, you're wearing something that exists only once in the world.

Design by Latife Nesrin · Made in Istanbul · Sold in London
